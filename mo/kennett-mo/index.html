Kennett, MO Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Kennett is a city in and the county seat of Dunklin County, Missouri, United States. The city is located in the southeast corner (or 'Bootheel') of Missouri, 4 miles (6.4 km) east of Arkansas and 20 miles (32 km) from the Mississippi River. It has a population of 10,932 according to the 2010 Census. Kennett is the largest city in the Bootheel, a mostly agricultural area.
